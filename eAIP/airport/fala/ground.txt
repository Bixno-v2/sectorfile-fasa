===== FALA Ground and Apron =====

* Apron is not really required on VATSIM, and only assigns parking bays and does clearances

* There are two run-up bays on RWY 07 and RWY 25 for GA aircraft.

==== Push-back Procedures ====

=== Phraseology ===

<code>Safair 441, push and start is approved, face [DIRECTION], QNH [QNH]</code>

<code>ZS-SOP, startup approved, QNH [QNH], report ready for taxi</code>

=== Directions ===

^ Apron ^ Type ^ Facing ^ Remarks ^
| Apron A | All | South | - |
| Apron B | All | East/West | - |
| Apron C | All | South | Smaller planes here only need startup |


==== Taxi Out Procedures ====

  * The ground controller controls the taxiways both north and South

  * The ground controller offers push and start clearance. Apron only gives traffic and hazard information.

  * Pilots can expect the ground controller to transfer them to tower upon reaching RWY 07/25.

  * If runway crossing at A3/B3 must be coordinated with tower controller, or the pilot must be transferred over to tower for the cross.


=== Phraseology ===

IFR
<code>Safair 441 taxi S A to holding point A4, runway 07</code>

VFR
<code>ZS-FFE taxi right A, holding bay runway 07, report ready in the bay</code>
<code>ZS-FEE taxi right A, hold short runway 07</code>


==== Taxi In Procedures ====

=== Phraseology ===

<code>Safair 441 taxi A R</code>
