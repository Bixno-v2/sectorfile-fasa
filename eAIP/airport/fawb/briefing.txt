===== FAWB Briefing =====

==== VFR Traffic ====

=== General Procedures ===
On first contact with Wonderboom, VFR traffic is expected to report the following information:
  * Aircraft Type
  * Registration (if ATC callsign is not the aircraft registration)
  * Persons on Board
  * Fuel Endurance
  * In addition, **outbound traffic** should report:
    * Position on the aerodrome
    * Estimated elapsed time (referred to as "Elapsed Time")
    * Departure request (including flight plan information, training request, etc.)
  * In addition, **inbound traffic** should report:
    * Position and altitude
    * Inbound request (approach type, intentions to join the circuit, etc.)
