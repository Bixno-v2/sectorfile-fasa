===== FALE Departure =====

  * FALE_APP will assume full responsibility of departures out of Durban King Shaka.
  * There is no handoff from Tower, as this is an automatic handoff given by the Clearance.


==== Departure Procedures ====

  * FALE_APP should pay particular close attention for conflicts with inbound aircraft and traffic from other aerodromes. 
  * Standard Handoff Altitude to FAJA unit is FL150 for outbound aircraft.
